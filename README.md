# ⚽ Football Analytics & Tactical Portfolio

Welcome to my football data analytics portfolio. This repository contains a collection of tactical reports, interactive recruitment dashboards, and statistical case studies designed for professional football environments.

The core objective of these projects is to (get a jobat a football club) bridge the gap between rigorous data engineering and accessible, actionable insights for coaching staff, scouts, and recruitment departments.

---

## 📂 Featured Projects

### 1. 📐 Internal Audit & Set-Piece Review (MD1–MD13)
**Folder:** `Set_Piece_&_Review_FC_Nantes`
* **Objective:** A 2-hour strategic meeting audit driven by a direct request from the coaching staff to investigate set-piece (CPA) vulnerabilities. 
* **Context:** Evaluates the team's tactical DNA and ranking trajectory over the first 13 matchdays, specifically answering the coach's question regarding set pieces: *Is the current record due to variance/bad luck, or a fundamental structural flaw?*
* **Deliverable:** PDF Reports
* **Languages:** 🇬🇧 English | 🇫🇷 Français

### 2. 🎯 Opposition Analysis: FC Nantes vs AS Monaco
**Folder:** `Opp_Analysis_FC_Nantes`
* **Objective:** Match preparation data report for Matchday 14 against AS Monaco. 
* **Context:** Analyzes the opponent's performance variance, tactical DNA, and passing networks to forecast formations and disrupt their build-up progression. Includes individual profiles on high-impact players.
* **Deliverable:** PDF Reports 
* **Languages:** 🇬🇧 English | 🇫🇷 Français

### 3. 🔍 Strategic Market & Recruitment Analysis (Squad Clustering)
**Folder:** `Squad_Clustering_and_Recrutement_FC_Nantes`
* **Objective:** Identify systemic profile gaps within the current FC Nantes squad and leverage data-driven recruitment to propose undervalued, high-impact targets.
* **Methodology:** Used K-Means clustering on StatsBomb Top 5 European League data to categorize player archetypes and highlight critical squad needs (e.g., Playmaker, Creative Winger). 
* **Note on Format:** The heavy technical pipeline (cleaning, feature engineering) was intentionally extracted from this deliverable to provide a clean, streamlined reading experience for non-technical Data Department staff.
* **Deliverable:**  HTML
* **Languages:** 🇬🇧 English | 🇫🇷 Français

### 4. 🌍 The "Most Competitive League" Study
**Folder:** `League_Competitivity_Blog`
* **Objective:** A data journalism piece designed for public discussion (Reddit/Blogs) that challenges subjective football narratives with objective mathematical measures.
* **Context:** Investigates the cliché that the English Premier League is inherently the most competitive league by analyzing the distribution of unique winners, point variances, and standard deviations over the last decade for various league across the globe.
* **Deliverable:** Jupyter Notebook
* **Language:** 🇬🇧 English Only
